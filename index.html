The Artist

Four decades of living art

BD Jagadish Bananki is one of Bangalore's most respected sculptors, with a career spanning over 40 years. His work draws from the rich visual heritage of Karnataka, reinterpreting ancient forms through a contemporary sensibility.

From intimate figurative sculptures to large public installations, each piece carries the unmistakable mark of a craftsman who has dedicated his life to the dialogue between material and meaning.
